About Me

Professor and Associate Dean, Department of Computing

Hello, My name is GopalaKrishnan Sethumathavan, I'm a Directorate of Online & Distance Education. I've started my career as a Part-Time Lecturer in National College,Thiruchirapalli (01.08.1986 to 31.12.1989), later upgraded myself from Associate Lecturer to Senior Lecturer in Shanmugha College of Engineering,Thanjavur (03.01.1990 to 31.01.2005), then worked as a Assistant Professor in Sastra University,Thanjavur (01.02.2001 to 31.12.2009)

Total Work Experience as on Date (as on 18th September, 2019): 34 years and 4 months Now, I'm a Associate Dean, Department of CA and also Directorate of Online and Distance Education
